- Save button uses `form="edit-waypoint-form"` to submit the form from outside the scroll area

**User-Facing Changes:**
- Edit view now feels like a natural part of the same card, not a separate smaller form
- Green header area fills the top space where the photo hero normally sits
- Cancel/Save buttons are in the sticky bottom bar (same position as Save/Navigate on normal view)
- Back button is in the same position as the close button on the route card
