## 9. FAB (+) Button - Conditional Visibility

### Overview
The green FAB (Floating Action Button) for map settings now only appears when the map is actually visible.

### When FAB is Visible
- ✅ Map tab with no open panels or drawers
- ✅ Viewing a route with details collapsed (route visible on map)
- ✅ Find/Saved tabs with panel collapsed (map visible behind)

### When FAB is Hidden
- ❌ Find/Saved panels open (panel covers map)
- ❌ Route details panel open (panel covers map)
- ❌ Creating a route (handled separately in create mode)

### Technical Implementation
```tsx
{((activeTab === "map" && !drawerOpen) || 
  (activeTab === "map" && drawerOpen && !mobileRouteDetailOpen) ||
  ((activeTab === "find" || activeTab === "saved") && !mobilePanelOpen)) && (
  <MobileFabMenu ... />
)}
```
