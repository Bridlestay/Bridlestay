### 3. Elevation Chart — Komoot-Style Softer Aesthetic
**File:** `components/routes/elevation-profile.tsx`

**Changes:**
- Added SVG area fill path with soft green gradient beneath the elevation line (Komoot-style)
- Defined `linearGradient` with green color fading from 25% opacity to 5% opacity
- Increased line stroke width from `1.2` to `1.8` for bolder, more visible line
- Extended floating marker drop-lines from partial height to full chart height (`y2="100"`)
- Made drop-lines lighter and more subtle (`opacity="0.4"`, `strokeWidth="0.3"`)
- Removed `overflow-hidden` from chart container to prevent marker clipping at edges

**Lines modified:** 138-171, 397-435

**Why:** Original chart had thin, harsh lines with no area fill. Drop-lines were cut off and markers clipped at edges. New design matches Komoot's softer, more polished elevation profiles.

## Risk Assessment
**Low risk** — All changes are cosmetic UI/UX improvements with no business logic or database changes.

- No API changes
- No schema migrations
- No authentication/authorization changes
- No payment or booking logic touched
- Backward compatible (existing functionality preserved)

**Potential issues:**
- Weather tips are hardcoded logic — future iteration could integrate with external weather advisory APIs
- Masonry layout (`columns`) may have inconsistent rendering across very old browsers (IE11), but Padoq targets modern browsers
- Increased photo gallery image sizes (`width={600}`) may impact load time on slow connections — consider adding lazy loading in future
