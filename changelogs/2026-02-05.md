## 1. Prominent Route Statistics (OS Maps Style)

## 2. Removed Star Rating System

### Overview
Removed star ratings from routes to avoid disincentivizing properties whose routes may not have high ratings.

### Changes
- Removed star rating display from route header
- Removed rating count display
- Routes now focus on engagement metrics (Likes, Completions, Shares) instead

### Rationale
Star ratings can negatively impact properties if their associated routes receive lower ratings. The engagement-based metrics provide value indication without the negative implications.

## How to Use

### Viewing Route Details
1. Click any route pin on the map or from Find Routes
2. Route details panel opens on left (desktop) or bottom (mobile)
3. Large stats immediately visible at top
4. Scroll down to see Stays, Discussion, Waypoints/Hazards
5. Photos and author info at very bottom

### Interacting with Sections
- **Nearby Stays**: Swipe horizontally, click to view on map
- **Discussion**: Click card to open full discussion
- **Waypoints**: Click to see all waypoints
- **Hazards**: Click to report or view existing hazards
