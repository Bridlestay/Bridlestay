**Modified Files:**

**What Changed:**

- Timeline line now expands with waypoint content when expanded (Komoot style)
- Muted color palette for waypoint circles:
  - Start (S): Changed from `bg-green-500` to `bg-green-600`

**Technical Details:**

**User-Facing Changes:**
