#### 3. Timeline Layout Structural Fix (commit: 5482475)

**Modified Files:**
- `components/routes/waypoint-timeline.tsx`
- `components/routes/waypoint-card.tsx`

**What Changed:**
- Complete structural rework of timeline positioning to match Komoot reference
- Timeline container now has proper left padding (`pl-10` = 40px) for timeline space

- **Numbered circles**: `absolute left-[-40px] top-2` positions 32px circles to center on line
- **Distance labels**: `absolute left-[-32px] top-1 w-10` centers small distance text on line
- **Content area**: Starts directly without extra margin since structure handles spacing
- **Line styling**: Initially `w-[1px] border-l-[1.5px]`, later improved to `border-l-2` for better visibility

**Issue Fixed:**
- Previous iterations had misaligned circles, invisible timeline, and wrong label positions
- Timeline line now properly visible and aligned with numbered circles
- Distance labels appear ON the timeline between waypoints (not under circles)
- Expanded content properly expands the timeline vertically
- Clean, professional Komoot-style appearance achieved

**User-Facing Changes:**
- Timeline line is clearly visible running vertically through all waypoints
